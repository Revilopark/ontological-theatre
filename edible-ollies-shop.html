Edible Ollies & Radical Shoots grow specialty microgreens and source the finest artisan products for Nashville's most discerning chefs, bartenders, and home cooks. Our regenerative practices ensure that every leaf, every mushroom, and every drop of honey carries the full depth of its flavor.

We believe food should be grown with intention, harvested at its peak, and delivered with love. That's why we operate on a pre-order model — everything is fresh, nothing sits on a shelf.
