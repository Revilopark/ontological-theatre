📋 Course Description

This practicum is built on a single premise: everything is a graph. Your social network, your knowledge, and the AI tools you use are three overlapping graph layers — the Trinity Graph. This course teaches you to see those layers, navigate them, and use them to build real ventures.

You will not write a business plan. You will build a living knowledge graph, reason through real problems using 14 reasoning modes (runes), and ship something that matters — with AI as your co-architect.

This is not a lecture. It's a practicum. We build things. We ship things. Graph theory is our conceptual frame for everything.

💡 Course Philosophy

"The biggest opportunities are always structural gaps — disconnected clusters that should be connected."

This course exists at the intersection of graph theory, entrepreneurship, and artificial intelligence. We don't separate these disciplines because reality doesn't separate them. Your social network shapes what you know. What you know shapes what you can build. What you build reshapes your network.

By the end of this practicum, you will have a living, traversable knowledge graph that maps how your mind works — and how to extend it with AI. That graph is your real deliverable. Everything else is a side effect.
