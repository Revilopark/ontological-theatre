Best Practice

Pro Tips

  • 🧾
    Always order sample yardage before committing. Quality and hand can vary significantly between dye lots and suppliers — never assume.
  • 📐
    True cost = FOB + Shipping + Duty. At China's 145% tariff, a $12/yd fabric becomes $29.40 landed — more than double. Always model full landed cost before sourcing.
  • 🇰🇷
    Korea has 0% tariff under KORUS FTA — a major competitive advantage for technical and stretch fabrics. Ideal for innovative knit structures.
  • 💎
    For Xanadu-level beadwork: source loose beads via Toho or Fire Mountain, then send to skilled beaders (India or local LA artisans). Dramatic savings vs. pre-beaded yardage.
  • ⭐
    ISW Beverly Hills is worth the price premium. Their knowledgeable staff can identify alternatives, source to order, and their theatrical/couture stock is unmatched in LA.
