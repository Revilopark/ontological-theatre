Attendance via VanderBot

Attendance is tracked through our class WhatsApp bot, VanderBot. When you arrive to class, you'll receive a code to text to VanderBot. It will request your location — if you're within 200 meters of the building, you're checked in.

Location data is used solely for attendance verification and is not stored beyond the class session. Participation requires consenting to one-time location access via WhatsApp.
📄

Project Pages

App updates are now published directly to your project page at vanderbot.com/me/[yourname]/[yourproject] rather than GitHub. It will be private to you and accessible once you're logged into your VanderBot.com account.

🛟

Dashboard & Support

If you have issues accessing the bot, visit your dashboard at vanderbot.com, sign in with your first name and spirit animal as your password (not case sensitive), and tap "I'm stuck" for help.

vanderbot.com is a third-party platform used for this course. It operates outside Vanderbilt's SSO and IT infrastructure. Do not reuse passwords from other accounts.
