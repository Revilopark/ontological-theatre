Co-founded by Jeff Bailey and Jack Bailey, headquartered in Nashville with offices in NYC and LA. Tim Scallen serves as CEO (appointed May 2024). The firm grew headcount by 60% in 2024 alone, now fielding 11 managing directors across 4 offices with 30+ active engagements entering 2025.
