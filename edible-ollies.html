2 acres. 50 raised beds. One mission.

Grow the most beautiful, flavorful edible flowers in Tennessee. Connect Nashville's chefs and bartenders directly to the soil their garnishes grow in.

"We're not just growing food. We're growing soil. The food is the byproduct."

  • No synthetic fertilizers or pesticides — ever
  • Cover cropping between seasons
  • Compost-only fertility from local sources
  • Beneficial insect habitat in every bed
  • Building soil organic matter, year over year
  • Pollinator corridors connecting all 50 beds
