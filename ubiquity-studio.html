Publishing Pipeline

The workflow from creation to distribution and back. Eight stages, one loop.

1
✨ Create
Content generated in Ontological Theatre Studios — Characters, Sparks, Artifacts, Environments, Music, and Stories all originate here. The Trinity Graph fuels the creative engine.
Character Studio Spark Engine Artifact Forge AI Assistants
⏱ Ongoing — continuous creative output
2
🔧 Format
Adapt raw content for every channel. Resize images to platform specs, render video at correct resolutions, format manuscripts as EPUB3/PDF, master audio for streaming standards. One source, many outputs.
Adobe CC FFmpeg Calibre Vellum iZotope Topaz
⏱ 1–3 days per content batch
3
🏷 Metadata
Title, description, tags, categories, keywords, ISBNs for books, ISRC codes for music, UPC barcodes, copyright notices. Good metadata is discoverability — it's the silent salesperson.
Publisher Rocket Bowker ISBN ISRC Registry Yoast TuneBat
⏱ 2–4 hours per title
4
⚖️ Rights
Artiquity chain of title — every content piece signed with C2PA Content Credentials. Licensing terms defined (exclusive/non-exclusive, territories, duration). Royalty splits among collaborators locked in before distribution.
Artiquity C2PA SDK Adobe CAI DocuSign IPFS
⏱ 1–2 days for initial setup; ongoing for each piece
5
🚀 Distribute
Push to all selected channels simultaneously or in sequence. API integrations handle ebook delivery to Amazon KDP, music to DistroKid, art to Printful, social posts via Buffer/Later. The world goes everywhere at once.
DistroKid Draft2Digital IngramSpark Buffer Printful API Shopify
⏱ Hours to days depending on platforms
6
📊 Monitor
Track performance, revenue, and engagement across all channels in one dashboard. Sales reports from Amazon, stream counts from Spotify, engagement from social platforms. Data flows back into the system.
Google Analytics BookReport Chartmetric Sprout Social Looker Studio
⏱ Ongoing — weekly reviews recommended
7
⚡ Optimize
A/B test cover art and descriptions. Adjust pricing dynamically. Reallocate marketing spend to highest-performing channels. Prune underperformers. Double down on what's working. Iterate fast.
Amazon A/B Test Koji Convertkit Facebook Ads BookBub
⏱ Monthly strategic reviews; weekly tactical tweaks
8
🔁 Feedback
Performance data feeds back into the Trinity Graph. Audience signals shape new Characters. Top-performing Sparks inspire new Stories. The loop closes — distribution intelligence becomes creative intelligence. The World evolves.
Trinity Graph API Artiquity Custom Webhooks Zapier
⏱ Continuous — the loop never ends

🔏 C2PA Content Credentials

Every piece of content distributed through Ubiquity Studio is signed with C2PA Content Credentials via the Artiquity protocol. This embeds cryptographic provenance data directly into the media file — who created it, when, with what tools, and under what license. Credentials survive format conversion and platform uploads. Consumers and platforms can verify authenticity instantly.
