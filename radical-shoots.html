Meet the Farm

Radical Shoots is a Nashville-based regenerative farm committed to growing the finest microgreens, crafting artisan cheeses from our own grass-fed herd, and partnering with local producers for raw honey, maple syrup, and gourmet mushrooms.

Everything we do is rooted in integrity — from seed to plate. We believe that real food, grown with care and sold with honesty, can nourish both people and the land.

We're proud to be part of Nashville's local food community, serving home cooks, chefs, and anyone who believes food should taste like something.
