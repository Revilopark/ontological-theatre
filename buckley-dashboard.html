📋 Regulatory Environment

The most powerful regulatory tailwinds in HVAC history are creating mandatory demand across Buckley's territory.

⏳ Regulatory Timeline

January 1, 2026 — IMMEDIATE

AIM Act: R-410A Manufacturing Cutoff

R-410A manufacturing/import ends. Mass Save removing from QPL. Transition to R-32 and R-454B (A2L mildly flammable). Installation deadline Dec 31, 2026 (extended to 2028 for pre-permitted).

Active Now — HIGH URGENCY

Boston BERDO

Buildings >20,000 sq ft must meet escalating emissions limits. HVAC = #1 compliance lever (>40% of building energy). $1,000/day penalties for non-compliance.

Early 2026

ASHRAE 90.1-2022 State Adoption

Fan Energy Index (FEI) now mandatory. ~10% reduction in allowed fan power. Power threshold lowered to 1 kW. 19% energy cost reduction vs 2019 edition.

Active — MA Municipalities

MA Three-Tier Energy Code

Stretch Code: 301/351 municipalities (~90% of population). Specialized Opt-In Code: 56 communities (32% of population) — effectively mandates all-electric new construction.

2030 Deadline

NYC Local Law 97

Covers ~57,000 buildings in Buckley's Eastern NY territory. Dramatically stricter limits effective 2030. ~63% of covered buildings projected to exceed thresholds.

Active — Incentive Program

Mass Save & MassCEC

Commercial heat pump/VRF rebates (2025–2027 plan). HEAT Loan: 0% interest up to $25K. Goal: 1 million heat pumps by 2030.

Endorsed Nov 2024

ASHRAE 241 — IAQ Standard

Control of Infectious Aerosols, endorsed by EPA/CDC. Codifies ventilation, filtration, and UV-C requirements. Directly impacts ERV, filtration, and ionization product demand.
